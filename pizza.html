pizza principles

shape

Circular, square, cylindrical (a.k.a. deep dish), we don't really care. Good pizza is good. Bad pizza is bad. The shape doesn't matter. We care far more about the size than the outline.

slicing paradigm

Some people prefer the traditional slice shape, while others like square-cut. We've experimented a great deal and found that the slicing pattern doesn't affect the taste in any way and therefore does not matter.

nicknames

A pizza is a pizza. Don't call it a "pie." That's something else. Definitely don't call it "za." That's stupid. Call it a pizza.

pineapple

Some people like it. Some people don't. Why are you wasting any part of your short life arguing about this?
